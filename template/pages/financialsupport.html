financial support

help our mission

The United States PostgreSQL Association provides financial support for a number of United States PostgreSQL activities - this is only made possible through the contributions of our fantastic community.

Please consider signing up as a sponsor.

We also accept donations through PayPal or with a credit card directly. If you would like to donate using a credit card, please contact treasurer@postgresql.us and we would be happy to generate an invoice for you. If you have any questions or concerns regarding PayPal donations (or if you have any suggestions for ways to donate), please contact us.

If your employer participates in the Benevity Workplace Giving Program, you can make donations to PgUS through payroll withholding. Some companies provide a matching amount as well! More information about Benevity can be found at www.benevity.com.

We are also registered with Nonprofit Connect (yourcause.com), which operates a similar workplace giving program for one-time or recurring donations, along with the option for an employer match.

a helping hand

If you are a PostgreSQL community-recognized user group, community-recognized conference, or educational speaker needing financial assistance, we may be able to provide financial sponsorship or other support. Our assistance extends beyond the US to user groups, conferences, and speakers anywhere that we, as a US non-profit corporation, are legally able to provide assistance. If you are in a region which has a community-recognized PostgreSQL organization which offers financial support, we recommend you contact them with your initial request before contacting PgUS.

Our assistance includes but is not limited to:

  • supporting the needs of PostgreSQL user groups
  • providing funds for educational speakers to attend events, either in-person or virtually
  • assisting with trade show representation supplies
  • covering the costs for swag creation (t-shirts, flyers, educational materials, etc.)

Those receiving financial sponsorship from PgUS maintain complete independence, are free to receive financial sponsorship from other entities, and to unassociate themselves from PgUS at any time.

Those wishing to obtain our services are requested to fill out a formal application. Please email contact@postgresql.us to receive the application and further instructions. Please note the following:

  • Name & purpose
  • What services are needed
  • The liason for communicating with PgUS
