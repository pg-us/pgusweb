Join PgUS

Through your membership to PgUS, you influence the growth and education of PostgreSQL. Memberships are $25 for professionals and $10 for students. You'll receive our monthly newsletter about what's happening at PgUS and you'll be able to vote in the annual PgUS elections. Join today!


Attend a User Group

PgUS sponsors more than 20 Postgres user groups around the world. Learn more about starting a new user group, or find an existing user group near you.


Sponsorships

When your company sponsors PgUS it sends a clear message that they care about the PostgreSQL community. Review our information about the benefits of signing up as a sponsor.


Donations

We also accept donations through PayPal or with a credit card directly. If you would like to donate using a credit card, please contact treasurer@postgresql.us and we would be happy to generate an invoice for you. If you have any questions or concerns regarding PayPal donations (or if you have any suggestions for ways to donate), please contact us.

If your employer participates in the Benevity Workplace Giving Program, you can make donations to PgUS through payroll withholding. Some companies provide a matching amount as well! More information about Benevity can be found at www.benevity.com.

We are also registered with Nonprofit Connect (yourcause.com), which operates a similar workplace giving program for one-time or recurring donations, along with the option for an employer match.
